About Us

Our Story

Zestify was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: make great food accessible to everyone, delivered fast and fresh. What started as a small team in one city has grown into a platform connecting 50,000+ customers with over 200 restaurants every single day.

What We Do

We partner with the best local restaurants and independent chefs to bring you a curated selection of food — from quick comfort meals to chef-crafted specials. Every restaurant on our platform is vetted for quality, hygiene, and consistency.

Our Promise

  • Delivery in 30 minutes or your next order is free
  • Real-time GPS tracking on every order
  • 24/7 customer support via chat and phone
  • 100% secure payments with bank-grade encryption
  • No hidden fees — the price you see is what you pay

Our Team

We're a team of 120+ food lovers, engineers, and logistics experts working every day to make your meal arrive hot and on time. Our riders are full-time employees with fair pay, health benefits, and flexible scheduling.

Contact Us

Have questions or feedback? Reach us at hello@zestify.com or call 1-800-ZES-TIFY. We're available Monday–Sunday, 8 AM–11 PM.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, address, payment details), information generated by your use of our services (order history, location data, device information), and information from third parties (social login providers, payment processors).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To provide real-time delivery tracking
  • To send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • To improve our platform and personalise recommendations
  • To prevent fraud and maintain platform security

3. Data Sharing

We share your data with restaurants (to prepare your order), delivery riders (name and delivery address only), and payment processors (to complete transactions). We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Order history is retained for 7 years for legal and tax compliance. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time. Contact us at privacy@zestify.com to exercise these rights.

6. Security

We use industry-standard TLS encryption, tokenised payment processing, and regular security audits to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, but we take every reasonable measure to safeguard your information.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and preferences, and optional analytics cookies to improve our service. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@zestify.com.
