About Us

Our Story

Who We Are

Zestify was founded in 2021 with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to restaurant-quality food, delivered with care. We partner with over 200 local restaurants to bring you the freshest, most delicious meals, right to your door in 30 minutes or less.

From family-run street-food kitchens to fine-dining establishments, every restaurant on our platform is vetted for quality, hygiene, and consistency. We're not just a delivery service — we're a community built around the love of great food.

Our Mission

To connect people with the food they love, while supporting local restaurants and building sustainable communities. We believe that a great meal can turn an ordinary day into something memorable.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Speed: Average delivery time of 28 minutes, tracked live end-to-end.
  • Quality: Every restaurant partner goes through a rigorous on-boarding process.
  • Support: Real humans available 24/7 — not chatbots.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging mandated across all partner restaurants.

Our Numbers

We serve 50,000+ happy customers across 12 cities, have partnered with 200+ restaurants, and maintain a 4.9-star rating across more than 80,000 reviews. We're proud of every single one.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Zestify, we collect information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account data: name, email address, and password (hashed and salted).
  • Order data: delivery address, order history, and payment method (processed by our certified payment provider — we never store card numbers).
  • Usage data: device type, browser, IP address, and in-app interaction logs to improve performance and debug issues.
  • Location data: only when you grant permission, to calculate delivery times and show nearby restaurants.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely to:

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Send order confirmations, receipts, and delivery updates.
  • Improve our platform through aggregate, anonymized analytics.
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Restaurant partners — to fulfil your order (name and delivery address only).

  • Law enforcement — when legally required.

4. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. Order history is retained for 3 years for legal and accounting purposes. You may request deletion at any time by contacting privacy@zestify.com.

5. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 at rest, regular penetration testing, and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we take every reasonable precaution.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also object to processing or request a portable copy of your data. To exercise these rights, email privacy@zestify.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will notify you via email and update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of Zestify after a change constitutes acceptance.
