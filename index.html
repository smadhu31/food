Who we are

Zestify was born in 2022 with a simple belief: great food should reach your door without drama. We partner with local restaurants who care about quality and connect them with hungry people in minutes. We're a small, passionate team of food lovers and engineers spread across India.

Our mission

Make every meal feel like it was cooked just for you, no matter where you are. We work closely with restaurants to maintain quality, train our delivery partners for speed and care, and build technology that keeps you in the loop at every step.

Why Zestify?

  • Live order tracking so you're never left wondering
  • Curated restaurant partners — we only list places we'd eat at
  • No hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay
  • Juices, meals, desserts — everything under one roof
  • 24/7 support from real humans, not bots

Our story

It started with a late-night craving and a 90-minute wait. Our founder Madhu decided there had to be a better way. Two years later, Zestify serves thousands of meals daily across multiple cities — but our promise remains the same: fast, honest, and delicious.

We collect information you provide when creating an account, placing orders, or contacting support — including your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and payment details. We also collect device information and usage data to improve our service.
